Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Our Priority

Last Updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Elimu Pepe. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and share your data when you interact with our services.

This policy applies to visitors to our website, registered users of the Elimu Pepe learning platform, parents or guardians of underage users, and institutional clients.

2. Our Role in Protecting Your Data

Elimu Pepe acts as a Data Controller. We are responsible for deciding how and why your personal data is processed. We operate under:

  • The Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019
  • The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), where applicable

3. When We Collect Personal Data

We collect personal data when:

  • You register, request a demo, or use our services.
  • You contact us through customer support.
  • You give consent to receive marketing communications.
  • Data is collected automatically through your device or browser (cookies/IP).

4. Types of Data We Collect

Identification and Contact Data:

Full name, Email address, and Phone number.

User Account Data:

Username or login credentials, settings, and preferences.

Payment and Financial Information:

When you make payments through Elimu Pepe, we collect M-Pesa transaction information (transaction ID and phone number) and payment history via our mobile payment gateway.

Usage Data:

Platform activity, session logs, time spent on the platform, and metadata associated with media uploads.

5. Why We Use Your Data

We use your data for several legal grounds:

  • Contract: To provide and manage your account.
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our platform and offer customer support.
  • Consent: To send marketing communications.
  • Legal Obligation: To meet regulatory requirements.

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@loholearning.co.ke. You may also object to automated decisions or withdraw consent at any time.

7. Children's Data

Important for Parents and Guardians: Elimu Pepe is built for K–12 learners, all of whom are considered minors.

  • Users under 18 must have verifiable parental or guardian consent before using the platform.
  • We handle children's data in strict compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019) and GDPR Article 8.
  • Parents can access, review, or request deletion of their child’s data at any time.

8. How We Protect Your Data

We implement encrypted data transfers using TLS/SSL, secure cloud infrastructure, and strict internal access controls. While we do our best to protect your data, no system is 100% secure.

9. Third Parties We Work With

We may share your data with trusted service providers who host our servers or process payments (e.g., M-Pesa). Where data is transferred outside Kenya, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.
