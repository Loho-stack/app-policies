1. Introduction

Loho Learning Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Elimu Pepe mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information about users of our App, including parents, guardians, and children.

Elimu Pepe is an educational platform designed to support Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum (CBC). Our App is intended for use by learners of all ages, including children under the age of 13, under parental or guardian supervision.

By downloading or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Full name and profile information (parent/guardian or learner)
  • Phone number (used for Safaricom carrier billing / subscription authentication)
  • School name and grade/class level
  • Account credentials (username and password, if applicable)

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Learning progress and quiz scores (used to personalise and track educational outcomes)
  • App usage data: pages visited, features used, session duration
  • Log data: IP address, timestamps, error logs

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Subscription status and billing data from Safaricom (via Direct Carrier Billing). We do not store full payment details.
  • Analytics data from Facebook Pixel on our web platform (not within the App itself)

We do NOT collect: precise GPS location, contacts, camera/microphone data, or financial account details.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Process and verify your Safaricom subscription
  • Personalise the learning experience based on grade level and progress
  • Track and display learning achievements, badges, and leaderboard rankings
  • Send important service notifications (e.g. subscription renewal, account alerts)
  • Improve App performance, diagnose technical issues, and conduct internal analytics
  • Ensure the safety and integrity of the platform
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties or use it for targeted advertising within the App.

4. Children's Privacy (Under 13)

Elimu Pepe is used by learners including children under the age of 13. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children. The following safeguards apply:

  • Children's accounts are created and managed by a parent or guardian who accepts this Privacy Policy on their behalf.
  • We collect only the minimum data necessary for educational and subscription purposes (name, school, grade, learning progress, and device info).
  • We do not serve behavioural or targeted advertising to children.
  • We do not allow children to publicly post personal information.
  • Parents or guardians may request access to, correction of, or deletion of their child's data at any time by contacting us at support@loholearning.co.ke.

This section is intended to comply with the Google Play Families Policy, COPPA (U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), and relevant Kenyan data protection law.

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Safaricom Limited to verify and process your subscription via Direct Carrier Billing.
  • Cloud hosting and infrastructure providers to store and process data securely on our behalf.
  • Analytics providers in aggregated, anonymised form to understand usage patterns.
  • Legal authorities if required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

We require all third-party service providers to handle your data securely and in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specifically:

  • Account and profile data: retained while your account is active.
  • Learning progress data: retained for the duration of the account and for up to 12 months after account deletion to enable re-enrolment.
  • Subscription and billing records: retained for up to 7 years for legal and financial compliance.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time (see Section 9).

7. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement appropriate technical and organisational measures, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Access controls limiting who within our organisation can access personal data
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure

While we strive to protect your personal data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials confidential.

8. Cookies and Tracking

The Elimu Pepe App uses a WebView to display content from our website. Our website uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: required for the App and website to function correctly.
  • Performance Cookies: help us understand which pages are most visited and how users navigate.
  • Functional Cookies: enable personalisation and enhanced features.

Our website also uses Facebook Pixel for marketing analytics on the web platform. This tracking is limited to our website and is not used to target children within the App.

You can manage cookie preferences at any time through your browser or device settings.

9. Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, and applicable international standards, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing
  • Data portability: receive your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing
